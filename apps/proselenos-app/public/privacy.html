Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

EverythingEbooks is a reading and writing application that respects your privacy and gives you complete control over your data. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Configuration and Settings

The app stores two types of configuration files:

  • General settings: Your preferences like selected AI model, author name, and current working EPUB
  • Sensitive settings: API keys for AI services are stored locally in your web browser

2. AI Services and API Keys

If you choose to use AI-powered features:

  • You provide your own API key for services on OpenRouter
  • These keys are encrypted and stored privately
  • Only our servers can decrypt these keys when you request AI assistance
  • Chat conversations with AI can be saved to your storage

3. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • All operations: All processing is client side, using your browser's storage.
  • Storage: All storage is client side, in your browser's storage, there is no cloud or database storage, and no telemetry.

4. What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell or share your personal information
  • We don't use your content to train AI models, but OpenRouter may so check their privacy/terms.
  • We don't track your writing or analyze your content
  • We don't knowingly collect information from children.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Portability: Your manuscripts are in standard EPUB formats you can use anywhere

6. Browser Storage

We use browser storage for:

  • Remembering your preferred text-to-speech voice setting
  • Bookmarks and other settings
  • This contains no personal information
  • All of your manuscript text and metadata, so be careful sharing your web browser with others

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy when we make changes.
